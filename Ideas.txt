*** Clubdevous ***

- General
  * Club + Rendezvous = Clubdevous
  * Free with ads or charge?
  
- An app where people can:
  * Add friends as clubbing friends
  * See where those friends are going
  * See friends arriviing/leaving clubs using GPS (must be enabled) 
  * Vote to decide where to go
  * See list of trending clubs
  * View graphs to see which clubs are hottest on which nights
  * Follow a particular DJ
  * Review clubs in general
  * Review clubs on particular nights
  * See what music is generally played at a certain club
  * See bottle prices etc. and special offers
  * Post party pictures and videos they want hidden from facebook
  
- Get revenue from ads from nightclubs
  * Nightclubs promote with special prices
  * Special offers given when using app while paying for drinks
  
- Add compatibility with Facebook
  * Automatically add friends who use Clubdevous as clubbing friends
